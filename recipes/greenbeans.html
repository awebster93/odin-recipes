Austin's Green Bean Recipe

This is my favorite recipe for green beans

I like green beans and this is one of the best recipes I know.

Ingredients

  • Green beans
  • soy sauce
  • garlic
  • butter
  • sriracha chili sauce

Steps

  1. Mix soy sauce, garlic, and sriracha together/li>
  2. Cook green beans for 3 minutes in the pan
  3. Add the sauce and cook for 1 minute on high
