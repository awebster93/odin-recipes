Austin's Fried Chicken Recipe

This is my favorite recipe for southern fried chicken

Everyone that has tasted southern fried chicken knows the magic of that tangy, spicy, crunchy goodness. I grew up eating the chicken from my mom and aunts, and it carries a special place in my heart. Follow this recipe or try your own modifications.

Ingredients

  • 8 chicken drumsticks, thighs, or combination
  • flour
  • sal and pepper
  • hot sauce
  • a big appetite

Steps

  1. Soak the chicken in hotsauce overnight
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste
  3. Coat the chicken in flour
  4. Fry the chicken until it is done
